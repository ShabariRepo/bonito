# Call Prep — Elliot Cohen (2026-04-21)

## TL;DR — What You Need to Know Going In

**Who he is:** Co-Founder at Foundations (sellwithfoundations.com) — a GTM consultancy ("revenue incubator") for early-stage tech founders. 11-50 employees, Toronto. Background in Series A/B fintechs.

**What he probably wants:** Either to sell you GTM services (you = his client), or to distribute Bonito to his portfolio companies, or both.

**Your one job on this call:** Figure out which one it is.

**The question that cuts through it:**
> "Are you looking at Bonito as something to distribute to your portfolio companies, or are you thinking about helping us build our GTM — or something else entirely?"

**If he pitches a retainer** — he's selling TO you, not FOR you. Say "interesting, let me think about it." Don't commit.

**If he talks commission/distribution** — that's the real opportunity. Ask for specifics on his portfolio and their AI spend.

## Deal Structures If It Gets to Numbers

| Structure | His Cut | How It Works |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Commission-only | 10-20% of contract value | He sources and closes. No salary, no equity |
| Finder/referral | 5-10% of first-year revenue | He introduces, you close |
| Channel/reseller | 20-30% margin | He buys wholesale, resells. Complex for early stage |
| GTM co-founder | 5-15% equity | No salary, owns sales motion. Only if fully committed |

**What to offer (only after he names his model):**
- 20% year-one contract value on deals he sources and closes
- 10% on renewals
- Paid on cash received, not on signing
- No exclusivity unless $500K pipeline in 6 months
- 90-day out clause for both sides

**Negotiation guardrails:**
- If 20-30% reseller ask — counter with 10-15% commission + milestones
- If equity ask — anchor at 1-2% max, 4-year vest, tied to revenue milestones
- Don't mix sales commissions with fundraising referral fees
- Don't commit to exclusivity without demonstrated pipeline

## GTM-as-a-Service Industry Context

- Legitimate category but quality varies enormously
- "Revenue incubator" is marketing, not an established term
- His revenue likely comes from founders paying for GTM help ($10-20K/mo retainers)
- His clients do $25-500K ACV deals themselves

**Critical distinction:**
- Distribution partner = makes money when YOU make money. Aligned.
- GTM consultancy = makes money whether you close or not. Less aligned.

**Red flags for this space:**
- Upfront fees with vague deliverables
- Equity + high retainer (double-dipping)
- Long lock-in (6-12 months) with no performance exit
- Claims 50+ clients with small team

**Before committing to anything:**
- Request 3+ client references and call them
- Check Ontario business registry
- Search Reddit/Twitter/Trustpilot for mentions
- Ask in founder communities (YC Slack, Indie Hackers)

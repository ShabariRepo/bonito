## The Problem

Creative production is a moving target.

Every campaign starts at zero. New briefs, new asset requirements, new rounds of back-and-forth with the agency. You wait days for first looks, weeks for revisions, and by the time something gets to market, the moment has often shifted.

AI tools promised to speed this up. In practice they introduced new problems. Each tool has its own interface, its own quirks, and none of them know Peller — your voice, your visual grammar, your audience. The output looks generic. The brand starts to feel like everyone else's brand.

The result: more creative speed is theoretically available, but unlocking it requires either becoming an AI expert yourself or trusting a tool that doesn't know your brand.

---

## What We're Building

A guided creative app — built for Peller specifically — that lets your team produce on-brand campaign assets in minutes, step by step, with no AI expertise required.

Your brand voice, visual style, and strategic principles are captured once. From then on, every campaign starts from that foundation. Your team works through a guided flow: choose a campaign type, pick from a curated library of prompts your agency has tuned to your brand, generate variations, mark the ones you love, and publish.

Every asset gets tracked. Every favorite gets remembered. Every published campaign teaches the system what works for Peller — so the next campaign starts smarter than the last.

## How It Works

The app is structured around the way creative production actually happens at a brand. Six layers, but you only interact with the top three. The strategic foundation underneath is built and maintained for you.

### 1. Brand Rules *(set once)*
Your tone, voice, visual grammar, and distinctive brand assets — captured in a structured profile the system uses on every generation. Set up at onboarding, refined over time.

### 2. Prompt Library *(curated for you)*
A growing library of reusable prompts tuned to the Peller brand. Each prompt is a starting point: "summer Niagara vineyard hero shot," "wine and food pairing carousel," "harvest behind-the-scenes." Your team picks one, customizes it if needed, and goes.

### 3. Insight Engine *(running quietly in the background)*
Automated research and cultural scanning — what's trending in wine, what your audience is reacting to — feeds suggestions into your campaign briefs.

### 4. Multi-Tool Orchestration *(handled for you)*
The system routes each asset type to the right AI model. Photography-style hero images go to one model, product shots to another, video to a third. You don't pick the tool. You pick the result you want.

### 5. Production Pipeline *(where you live)*
Your team's main interface. Submit a brief, watch it generate, review variations side by side, mark favorites, publish or hand off to design.

### 6. Creative QA + Human Taste *(yours)*
Your team always has the final say. Every asset is reviewed and approved before it ships. The system makes the work faster — you keep the creative judgment.

## The Three Features That Make This Different

Most AI tools are one-shot generators. You get an image, you save it, you move on. There's no memory, no learning, no compounding value.

This app is built around three connected systems that compound over time:

### Brand Rules — the foundation
Your brand is captured as structured rules the system follows on every generation. Tone, palette, voice, visual constraints. No more "AI-generated but generic." Every output starts on-brand.

### Prompt Library — the institutional memory
Every prompt that ever worked for your brand becomes a reusable starting point. Your team builds a vocabulary over time. New marketers onboard fast because the prompts are right there, waiting.

### Performance Loop — the learning system
Every asset gets a unique ID. You mark the ones you love internally. Once campaigns go to market, you upload performance data — engagement, reach, conversions — and the system shows you which prompts and styles delivered the best results. Over six months, you have a performance-weighted library of what actually works for Peller — not what works in general.

This third piece is the one that makes the app valuable a year from now. Without it, you have a generation tool. With it, you have a creative intelligence system that gets better the more you use it.
